Abraham's Breakfast Sandwich:

Ingredients:

    1 Kaiser bread rolls
    2 slices of swiss cheese
    2 slices of ham
    2 Large eggs
    Mayo to spread onto bread
    Some kind of oil to coat bottom of pan
    Bacon bits (optional)




Steps:

  1. Cut bread into two halves
  2. Spread small amount of mayo on both halves of the bread. Just enough to cover the surface
  3. Put one slice of Ham on the bottom half
  4. Put one slice of Swiss Cheese on top of that ham
  5. Crack both eggs into a bowl and whisk well
  6. Put Bacon bits into bowl with eggs and mix (add as much as you think you should)
  7. Add oil to pan and heat at max until hot
  8. Add contents of bowl to pan
  9. Flip eggs with spatula once one side is cooked
  10. After other side is cooked, fold sides of the omelette into itself to create a square from the circle
  11. Place folded omelette onto the bottom half of the bread
  12. Put another slice of Swiss Cheese on the omelette
  13. Put another slice of Ham on top of the Swiss Cheese
  14. Place top half of bread on top of everything
  15. Put the finished sandwich in a panini press
  16. Take out once the pieces of bread are nice and hard (you can check with a knife to see how much it bends)
  17. Cut in half, and enjoy!
